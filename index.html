About Elevate

Care that treats the whole person, not just the presenting problem.

We started Elevate because too many people wait until they're in crisis to ask for support. Our clinicians blend talk therapy with practical wellness coaching, so sessions leave you with language for what you're feeling and tools you can actually use between appointments.

Whether you're navigating life transitions, stress, trauma, grief, and emotional overwhelm. We draw from a variety of evidence-based approaches and tailor treatment to meet each client’s unique needs and goals.

Services

Support for wherever you're starting from

Every plan is built around your goals, not a template. Here's where most clients begin.

Individual therapy

Anxiety, depression, life transitions, self-esteem, and burnout — a steady space to think out loud and build a plan forward.

Adult counseling

Communication, trust, intimacy, and repair — grounded in Gottman-informed methods that help you argue better, not just less.

Teen & young Adult counseling

Identity, school stress, family dynamics, and the pressure of growing up online — with parents kept informed, not shut out.

Wellness coaching

Habits, mindfulness, and stress management for clients who want structure and accountability alongside — or instead of — therapy.
